Hi!

I’m a PhD student in philosophy at Stockholm University. I work on semantics, pragmatics, and the philosophy of language. Much of my work has concerned question semantics, in particular inquisitive semantics.

I started as a PhD student in February, 2019. Before this, I obtained a Master’s degree in logic from University of Amsterdam in 2018, and a Bachelor’s degree in philosophy (with a minor in linguistics) from Stockholm University in 2016.

Since 2020 I am also associated with the project Knowledge Resistance, via my work on fragmented systems of belief.
